About Civil Air Patrol Service

As Cadet Commander of Heartland Composite Squadron, I provide strategic leadership and mentorship to our cadet corps. I oversee training programs, coordinate emergency services operations, and ensure the highest standards of discipline and professionalism.

My responsibilities include managing squadron operations, developing cadets into future leaders, and representing Civil Air Patrol's mission of aerospace education, emergency services, and cadet development.

Civil Air Patrol has shaped my leadership philosophy, aviation knowledge, and commitment to service. Every mission reinforces our core values: integrity, excellence, and dedication to our communities.
