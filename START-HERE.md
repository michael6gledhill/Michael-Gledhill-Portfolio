## ‚è±Ô∏è 3-Step Quick Start

### Step 1: Edit Your Profile (5 minutes)

Open the file: `data/profile.json`

Replace the template data with YOUR information:

```json
{
  "name": "YOUR NAME HERE",
  "rank": "YOUR RANK HERE",
  "unit": "YOUR SQUADRON HERE",
  "missionStatement": "Your leadership motto",
  "bio": "Write 100-150 words about yourself...",
  ...
}
```

**What to change:**
- `name` - Your full name
- `rank` - Your CAP rank
- `unit` - Your squadron/unit
- `missionStatement` - Your leadership quote
- `bio` - Your biography
- `experience` - Your promotions and positions
- `awards` - Your awards and achievements
- `activities` - Your service activities
- `skills` - Your leadership and technical skills

See `QUICK-REFERENCE.md` for all fields.

### Step 2: Add Your Photos (5 minutes)

Replace placeholder images in the `images/` folder:

1. **Hero Image** (`hero-placeholder.jpg`)
   - Size: 1920x1080px
   - Your best leadership photo

2. **Profile Photo** (`about-placeholder.jpg`)
   - Size: 600x600px (square)
   - Professional portrait

3. **Gallery Photos** (`gallery-1.jpg` through `gallery-8.jpg`)
   - Size: 800x600px
   - Activity/event photos (at least 4, up to 8)

**Tip**: Compress images at [TinyPNG.com](https://tinypng.com) before uploading.

### Step 3: Deploy to GitHub Pages (2 minutes)

1. Go to your repository on GitHub.com
2. Click **Settings** tab
3. Scroll to **Pages** section
4. Set:
   - Source: `Deploy from a branch`
   - Branch: `main`
   - Folder: `/ (root)`
5. Click **Save**

**Your site will be live at:**
```
https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/Michael-Gledhill-Portfolio/
```
